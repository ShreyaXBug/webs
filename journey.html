The idea of a flower bouquet website emerged from a passion for floral arrangements, elegance, and creativity. The goal was to provide a platform that offers beautifully curated flower bouquets, making gifting and decorating effortless.
Brainstorming Names The team explored multiple name ideas that reflected freshness, nature, and charm. Some of the early name ideas included:
Petal Bliss
Flora Haven
Bloom Essence
The Blossom Crate
The team wanted a name that was simple, aesthetic, and memorable while representing the beauty of flowers.


The Final Name: The Bloom Box
After several discussions, "The Bloom Box" was chosen because:
✓ "Bloom" represents the fresh, vibrant flowers.
✓ "Box" symbolizes packaging, making it perfect for a flower delivery concept.
✓ The name is catchy, easy to remember, and gives a premium feel.
